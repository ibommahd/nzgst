GST Calculator - New Zealand (NZ)

GSTstrong> which stands for Goods and Services Tax, is a 15% tax that is added to the price of many things you buy in New Zealand. This includes most items that are imported from other countries, as well as some services that are brought in from outside New Zealand.

Ever since Goods and Services Tax (GST) was introduced, many people in New Zealand (Kiwis) have to figure out how much GST they need to add to the price of goods or services. They also need to calculate how much GST is already included in the price, so they can claim tax credits for any GST included in the cost of goods, services, or anything else they acquire to use for their business.

How to use GST Calculator NZ?

When you type a number in the "Amount" box, you will see two results on the same page:

  • The portion of the price that is the GST amount and the price without the GST.

You can also adjust the GST percentage if it changes or if you want to calculate GST for different countries, like Australian GST (10%) or Malaysian GST (6%).

GST Calculations

How to calculate GST in New Zealand? Adding 15% to the price is simple (just multiply the amount by 1.15). But if you want to know how much GST was included in the price or find the price without GST, it can be a bit tricky:

    To find out how much GST was included in the price, you need to follow these steps:

  • Multiply the GST-inclusive price by 3.
    • Then, divide the result by 23. For example, if the GST-inclusive price is $230, the calculation would be: $230 * 3 / 23 = $30. To calculate the price without GST, follow this step:
  • Divide the original amount by 1.15.
    • For example, if the original price is $230, the calculation would be: $230 / 1.15 = $200. These calculations will help you understand the GST portion in the total price or determine the price before adding GST.

    Don't make the most common mistakes when dealing with percentages:
    $200 + 15% = $230, however $230 - 15% is NOT equal $200, actually it's $195.5!
